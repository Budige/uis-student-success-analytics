OIRE Key Insight (2023): UIS enrollment stabilized at 4,402 (+1.4% from 2022 trough), reversing a 7-year decline. Online enrollment at 56.1% leads all IL public universities. Graduation rate improved +3.6pp over 7 cohorts. Pell retention equity gap narrows but remains a 10.3pp challenge.
